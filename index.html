Upload your profile photo from your favorite social media platform to create a new photo stamped with the message "Immigrants Make America Great".

How to Download Your Profile Picture

Instagram (desktop):
  1. Open Instagram on desktop and go to your profile
  2. Right-click on your profile picture
  3. Select "Open Image in New Tab"
  4. Right-click the full-size image and select "Save Image As"
  5. Upload the saved image above
Instagram (mobile):

Unfortunately there isn't a great way to download your profile photo from Instagram. The easiest method of uploading a photo to this photo generator is if you still have an original copy of your profile photo. If not, try taking a screenshot of your profile photo and cropping it. Alternately, sites like instadp.com will download the photo for you.

Facebook:
  1. Go to your Facebook profile
  2. Click on your profile picture to open it
  3. Click the three dots (...) and select "Download"
  4. Upload the downloaded image above
